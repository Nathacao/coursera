Boteco do Brasil

In the heart of Brazilian gastronomy, Boteco do Brasil is more than just a place to eat — it's an experience for the senses. Our cuisine celebrates the diversity of flavors and textures, with dishes and portions ranging from classic to surprising. Each recipe is prepared with fresh, selected ingredients, guaranteeing quality and authenticity in every bite. Behind them there's a team of professionals passionate about the art of serving well, with attention to detail and a smile on their faces. All was carefully designed to combine comfort, charm, and a touch of refinement that transforms each visit into a special moment. Whether for a relaxed happy hour or a special dinner, Boteco do Brasil is your right destination.

It all began in 1997, when a couple passionate about the art of cooking, decided to turn their dreams into reality. Cooks at heart, they embarked on a journey through Brazil and the world, exploring markets, fairs, bars, and popular kitchens. Each stop was a discovery: exotic spices, ancestral recipes, stories told between pots and pans, and laughter. They sought more than just flavors—they wanted to understand the soul behind each dish, the living culture that pulsates in simple tables and sincere encounters. Inspired by this richness, they created Boteco do Brasil: a space where food is memory, affection, and celebration. The atmosphere reflects this mix: rustic and elegant, welcoming and vibrant, like a good conversation that has no end. The engaged team, lovingly trained, shares the same passion for serving well and making each customer feel at home. For this reason, Boteco do Brasil is more than a restaurant — it's a meeting point for those who value flavor, history, and the joy of living.

A dream has become true and now, each dish is a celebration of authentic flavors and irresistible textures, a true gastronomic journey through the delights of Brazil. Boteco do Brasil is a place where the magic is in the air: from the ambiance that was designed to offer comfort, charm, and that touch of refinement that makes all the difference, to the best food, prepared by the best professionals with the best ingredients and of course, with the best service, provided with friendliness, efficiency, and attention to detail. Each visit is a unique experience, where flavor and well-being go hand in hand. At Boteco do Brasil, tradition and excellence meet to enchant you!
